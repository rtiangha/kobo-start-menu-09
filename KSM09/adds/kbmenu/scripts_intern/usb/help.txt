<h3>USB mass storage:</h3>
<p>Connecting:</p>
<ul>
<li>press "usb enable.sh"</li>
<li>wait until the message "USB support is enabled" appears</li>
<li>connect to the PC</li>
</ul>

<p>Removing:</p>
<ul>
<li>eject</li>
<li>press "usb disable.sh"</li>
<li>disconnect from PC</li>
</ul>

<p>If you copy books to the reader you plan to read with nickel, connect to the pc while running nickel rather than doing so from here.</p>
<p><b>Only for emergency cases</b>: If /mnt is busy and you must urgently connect, try "scripts" > "tests" > "usb dirty"</p>
